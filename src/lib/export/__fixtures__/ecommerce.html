Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase and download books?

Simply browse our catalog, add books to your cart, proceed to checkout with your preferred payment method, and receive instant access to download. Your purchases are linked to your account for lifetime access.

What formats are available for digital books?

We offer books in multiple formats including ePub, PDF, MOBI, and HTML5. You can choose the format that best suits your reading device and preferences.

Do you offer refunds for digital purchases?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all digital purchases. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund, no questions asked.

Can I share books with family members?

Yes! Our Family Sharing plan allows up to 6 family members to access the same books with individual reading progress tracking. Premium plans include this feature at no additional cost.

How does the membership program work?

Members earn 1 point per dollar spent on book purchases. Accumulated points can be redeemed for discounts, exclusive titles, and early access to new releases. Higher membership tiers unlock additional benefits.
