FAQs

Common Questions

01.

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary by scope. A brand identity typically takes 3–4 weeks; a full website design and development engagement runs 8–14 weeks. We provide a detailed project roadmap with milestones before any work begins so expectations are clear from day one.

02.

What industries do you work with?

+

We partner with ambitious brands across technology, fintech, luxury consumer goods, healthcare, media, and e-commerce. Our team's diverse background means we bring industry-informed perspective regardless of your vertical, while keeping a fresh, unbiased creative eye.

03.

Do you offer post-launch support and maintenance?

+

Yes. All Professional and Enterprise projects include a 90-day post-launch support period. After that, we offer flexible retainer arrangements for ongoing design, development, and strategic support. We believe in building long-term partnerships, not one-off transactions.

04.

What does the collaboration process look like?

+

We use a collaborative sprint model. After discovery, we share weekly deliverables via a dedicated project workspace. You'll have direct access to your project lead at all times, and feedback loops are structured to keep things efficient without becoming chaotic. No surprises.

05.

How do you handle intellectual property and ownership?

+

Upon final payment, full intellectual property rights for all deliverables transfer to you. We retain the right to display the work in our portfolio unless an NDA is in place. We're completely transparent about this from day one — your business outcomes and IP protection always come first.
